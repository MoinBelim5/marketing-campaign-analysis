#  Marketing Campaign Analysis – Power BI

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes marketing campaign performance using **Power BI**, with a focus on **ROI, revenue efficiency, conversions, and channel effectiveness**.  
The objective is to convert dashboard metrics into **clear, business-driven insights** that support marketing and budget decisions.

---

##  Objectives
- Evaluate overall marketing performance using key KPIs  
- Identify profitable and underperforming campaigns  
- Compare performance across channels and regions  
- Analyze time-based trends in revenue and conversions  
- Deliver executive-ready insights, not just visualizations  

---

##  Key KPIs
- Return on Investment (ROI)  
- Conversion Rate  
- Click-Through Rate (CTR)  
- Total Revenue  
- Clicks & Conversions  

---

##  Dashboard Pages
### 1. Performance Dashboard
- KPI cards (ROI, Conversion Rate, CTR)  
- Channel-wise and region-wise performance analysis  
- Revenue vs conversion comparison  
- Campaign-level profitability view  

### 2. Executive Insights
- Consolidated business insights  
- Channel and regional performance summary  
- Trend analysis for revenue, clicks, and conversions  
- Action-oriented insights for decision-making  

---

##  Key Business Insights
- Overall marketing spend is **highly profitable**, with a strong ROI.  
- Around **75% of campaigns are profitable**, reflecting effective budget allocation.  
- **Radio** is the top-performing channel across ROI, revenue, and conversions.  
- Digital channels generate **high-intent traffic with strong conversion efficiency**.  
- Revenue does not always increase with clicks, emphasizing the importance of **revenue per click** over traffic volume.

##  Dataset
A **synthetic, campaign-level marketing dataset** containing budget, clicks, conversions, revenue, channels, regions, and time-based attributes.  
The data is used solely for analysis and portfolio demonstration purposes.
